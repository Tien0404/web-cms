About VGBC

The Vietnam Global Business Council (VGBC) empowers Vietnamese and international enterprises to connect, collaborate and grow sustainably. We operate as a strategic trade & business council, facilitating cross‑border partnerships, trade missions and market entry between Vietnam and the world.

Mission

Be the trusted gateway that enhances Vietnam’s global business presence—linking government, corporations and entrepreneurs through meaningful cooperation and high‑impact initiatives.

Vision

Position VGBC as Asia’s leading hub for international business promotion and partnership development—where language, integrity and innovation converge.

Core Values
  • Excellence & Integrity — International‑grade professionalism.
  • Bridging East & West — Multilingual experts turn communication into connection.
  • Empowerment & Growth — Measurable impact for sustainable expansion.
